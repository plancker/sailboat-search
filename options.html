Help

What is archiving?
By archiving a page, you can search with keywords from anywhere in the page. In contrast, history search requires keywords to be in the title.
How to archive?
Just click on the extension icon in the top right of your browser (while you're on that page). It should turn from black to green.
Where can I see the archived pages?
Just go to the "Sailboat Archive" folder in Other Bookmarks. PLEASE NOTE: Please don't add/delete pages directly here.
How can I unarchive a page?
Just click on the extension icon again (while you're on that page). It should turn from green to black.
What is automatic archiving?
If you spend more than x (default 120) seconds on a web-page (non-idle time), it will automatically archive it.
Who sees my data?
NO ONE, except you. Moreover, all the data is stored on your laptop.
